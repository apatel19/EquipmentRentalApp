Equipment Rental

Info about app

App idea is to make renting an equipment easy. Lot of time when we want to rent equipments we have to go to Home Depot, Lowes, or other equipment rental places.

But what if you can rent a same equipment from your neighbour? App also allows user to earn money by leting user post their equipments for others to rent it.
